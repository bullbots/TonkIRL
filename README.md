# TonkIRL
 Code Repo for our T-Shirt Cannon

## Control Documentation
 Before turning on the robot, turn on the controller. Wait at least a minute before pressing the enable button on the robot.

 Left Dial (CH8): sets the pressure of the firing tank between 20 psi on the far left and 50 psi on the far right. Leds on the robot will represent the pressure being used.
 Left Analog Stick: Controls forward/backward movement of the robot.
 Right Analog Stick: Controlls rotation (Left/Right) and Firing Angle (Up/Down).
 Right Shoulder Button (CH6): Fire Cannon.
